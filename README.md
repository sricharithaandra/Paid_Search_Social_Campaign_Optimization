**Paid Search & Social Campaign Optimization**

Multi-Channel Digital Marketing Campaign | Google Ads · Facebook Ads


Overview:
An 8-week multi-channel paid digital marketing campaign designed to promote Wright State University's MS in Marketing Analytics & Insights (MSMAI) program to prospective students in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The campaign ran across Google Search, Google Display, and Facebook with a total budget of $1,400.


Campaign Objectives:
Drive website traffic to the MSMAI program webpage.
Generate awareness among prospective students in Hyderabad, India. 
Maximize clicks and impressions within a fixed budget.
Test and optimize keyword bidding, audience targeting, and ad formats.


Tools:
- Google Ads: Search and Display campaign management.
- Facebook Ads: Paid post and video promotion.


Key Findings & Insights: 

- Audience: 18–24 age group drove the most clicks (479) and conversions (8) on Google Search.
- Channel Performance:
  - Google Search significantly outperformed Display in both volume and conversion quality.
  - Facebook delivered the best cost efficiency — $0.01–$0.03 CPC vs $0.20 on Google Search.
  - Facebook video exceeded expectations with 131K+ impressions at minimal cost.
- Optimization Impact: Performance improved week over week through keyword refinement and budget reallocation.


What We Would Do Differently: 
- Allocate more budget to Facebook given its superior cost efficiency.
- Invest earlier in Display campaign to allow more optimization time.
- Explore influencer marketing targeting student communities.
